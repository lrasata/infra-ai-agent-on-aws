Incident Response Playbook

An incident is any event that disrupts or degrades a production service.

Incident response follows four phases:

1. Detect and Triage
Identify the issue, determine severity, and confirm whether customer impact exists.

2. Mitigate
Stabilize the system by rolling back changes, scaling resources, or applying temporary fixes.

3. Communicate
Notify stakeholders, update status pages, and keep impacted teams informed.

4. Resolve and Review
Fully resolve the issue and conduct a post-incident review to identify root cause and follow-up actions.

For high-severity incidents, mitigation and communication should happen in parallel.
