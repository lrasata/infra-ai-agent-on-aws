Escalation Policy

Severity levels determine when and how incidents are escalated.

Severity 1:
- Customer-facing outage
- Immediate escalation required
- Page the on-call engineer and notify the service owner

Severity 2:
- Degraded performance or partial outage
- Notify the owning team
- Escalate if not resolved within 30 minutes

Severity 3:
- Minor issue with no customer impact
- Track during business hours
- No immediate escalation required

If the primary on-call engineer does not respond within 10 minutes, escalate to the secondary on-call or team lead.
